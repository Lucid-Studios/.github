OAN META-GOVERNANCE & CONSTITUTIONAL ENFORCEMENT MODULE
Constitutional Build Document v0.1
-------------------------------------------------------

Purpose:
--------
To provide a supervisory Constitutional Enforcement Layer (CEL) 
within the OAN Tech Stack. This module ensures invariant preservation 
across Law, Finance, and Entrepreneurial modules and enforces role, 
credential, and override governance.

This module does not execute business operations.
It enforces structural integrity.

Philosophical Foundation:
-------------------------
1. Law is the boundary.
2. Structure precedes authority.
3. Authority never precedes accountability.
4. Overrides must be visible.
5. Refusal is a governance tool.
6. No silent mutation of state.

-------------------------------------------------------
LAYER 0: CONSTITUTIONAL INVARIANTS
-------------------------------------------------------

Invariant 1: Law Supremacy
   - No module may bypass statutory compliance checks.
   - Legal hard stops cannot be silently overridden.

Invariant 2: Capital Partition Integrity
   - Personal, Operational, and Speculative capital partitions remain isolated.
   - Cross-partition reallocation requires logged authorization.

Invariant 3: Credential Gate Enforcement
   - Role-bound features unlock only after verified credentials.
   - Credential verification must be documented and timestamped.
   - No implied licensure is generated by the system.

Invariant 4: Human Authorization
   - Binding actions require explicit human confirmation.
   - Automation may not commit legal or financial obligations autonomously.

Invariant 5: Append-Only Audit Spine
   - All material decisions are logged.
   - Amendments are additive, never destructive.
   - Override events generate elevated log entries.

-------------------------------------------------------
ROLE ARCHITECTURE
-------------------------------------------------------

Operator
   - Default system user.
   - Receives awareness and structured assistance.
   - Must approve all binding actions.

Competent Agent
   - Verified credential holder in declared discipline.
   - May unlock role-bound drafting assistance.
   - Retains full professional liability.

Executive Authority (Board/CEO)
   - May authorize strategic overrides.
   - Cannot override statutory compliance invariants.
   - Override actions require justification log and review timer.

Meta-Governance Engine
   - Enforces invariant checks.
   - Triggers refusal where required.
   - Logs escalations.
   - Maintains authority separation.

-------------------------------------------------------
OVERRIDE PROTOCOL
-------------------------------------------------------

When override is initiated:

1. Identify invariant affected.
2. Require written justification.
3. Declare risk class.
4. Set review checkpoint timestamp.
5. Log override as elevated event.
6. Trigger cooling-off timer for high-risk cases.

High-risk overrides require:
   - Dual acknowledgment (Operator + Executive Authority)
   - Post-action review entry

-------------------------------------------------------
REFUSAL PROTOCOL
-------------------------------------------------------

When system detects violation risk:

1. Classify violation type (Legal / Financial / Governance)
2. Soft Denial (Explanatory Refusal)
   - Provide reasoning
   - Offer compliant alternative
3. Hard Stop (UPL or statutory violation)
   - Block artifact generation
   - Recommend licensed professional
   - Log refusal event
4. Escalation Option
   - Allow authorized review under credential gate

Refusal events are permanent log entries.
