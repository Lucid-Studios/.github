OAN ENTREPRENEURIAL GOVERNANCE MODULE
Constitutional Build Document v0.1
-----------------------------------

Purpose:
--------
To provide a structured, governance-first Entrepreneurial Governance Module (EGM) 
within the OAN Tech Stack. This module supports operators in forming, operating, 
and sustaining lawful enterprises through disciplined planning, compliance tracking, 
and engrammed decision logging.

The system does not replace the entrepreneur.
It scaffolds disciplined entrepreneurial action.

Philosophical Foundation:
-------------------------
1. Law precedes ambition.
2. Structure precedes scale.
3. Filing precedes expansion.
4. Cash flow precedes hiring.
5. Human accountability is never removed.
6. All material decisions are engrammed append-only.

--------------------------------------------------
LAYER 0: ENTREPRENEURIAL GOVERNANCE KERNEL (EGK)
--------------------------------------------------

Tone:
Institutional. Procedural. Compliance-aware.
No hype. No disruption rhetoric. No growth guarantees.

Core Functions:

1. Business Formation Pathway
   - Entity type comparison (sole prop, LLC, corp, partnership)
   - Jurisdiction tagging
   - Licensing requirement checklist
   - Registration milestone tracker
   - Initial compliance roadmap

2. Legal Awareness Layer
   - Contract necessity prompts
   - Risk exposure declaration
   - Insurance requirement modeling
   - Employment classification awareness
   - Regulatory dependency mapping

3. Filing & Deadline Governance
   - Filing calendar with jurisdiction tags
   - Tax filing schedule tracking
   - Renewal reminders (licenses, permits, filings)
   - Mandatory reporting alerts
   - Missed-deadline escalation logging

4. Operational Planning
   - Business goal declaration
   - Pathway decomposition (Milestone → Task → Subtask)
   - Revenue model articulation
   - Cost structure mapping
   - Hiring readiness assessment

5. Communication Discipline
   - Email template scaffolding
   - Public vs private communication separation
   - Stakeholder categorization
   - Decision record logging
   - Negotiation summary logs

--------------------------------------------------
ENGRAM REQUIREMENTS
--------------------------------------------------

Each entrepreneurial action must declare:

   - Objective
   - Legal context (jurisdiction)
   - Risk exposure estimate
   - Capital impact
   - Compliance impact
   - Human approval confirmation
   - Review timestamp

All entries append-only.
No retroactive mutation.
Corrections are logged as amendments.

--------------------------------------------------
SAFETY INVARIANTS
--------------------------------------------------

1. No business action may bypass compliance tracking.
2. Hiring requires classification confirmation.
3. Capital expenditure above threshold requires review log.
4. Regulatory risk must be acknowledged before proceeding.
5. All third-party agreements require documentation entry.

--------------------------------------------------
ENTREPRENEURIAL DISCIPLINE FRAMEWORK
--------------------------------------------------

The module enforces three pillars:

A. Stability
   - Business runway maintained
   - Personal and business finances separated
   - No speculative allocation from operational capital

B. Legality
   - Filing deadlines met
   - Required permits tracked
   - Transparent audit log maintained

C. Measured Growth
   - Expansion only after runway threshold met
   - Hiring only after revenue validation
   - Capital scaling only after risk modeling

--------------------------------------------------
RESEARCH OBJECTIVE
--------------------------------------------------

Primary Research Question:
Does engrammitized entrepreneurial governance reduce legal error, 
missed filings, and capital misallocation for bonded operators?

Metrics:
   - Missed filing reduction rate
   - Time-to-decision clarity
   - Compliance adherence percentage
   - Runway preservation delta

   - Revenue-to-expense stability ratio
