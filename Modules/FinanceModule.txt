OAN FINANCIAL GOVERNANCE MODULE
Constitutional Build Document v0.1
-----------------------------------

Purpose:
--------
To establish a deterministic, governance-first Financial Governance Module (FGM) within the OAN Tech Stack. 
The module prioritizes capital clarity, liquidity preservation, compliance awareness, and operator accountability.
Speculative activity is segregated into optional, risk-bounded extension kits.

Philosophical Foundation:
-------------------------
1. Stability precedes growth.
2. Liquidity precedes leverage.
3. Transparency precedes automation.
4. Human accountability is never removed.
5. All capital decisions are engrammed append-only.

--------------------------------------------------
LAYER 0: FINANCIAL GOVERNANCE KERNEL (FGK)
--------------------------------------------------

Tone:
Institutional. Black & white. Compliance-aware.
No hype. No performance promises.

Core Functions:

1. Cash Flow Modeling
   - Revenue projection (conservative, expected, optimistic)
   - Fixed vs variable expense modeling
   - Burn rate calculation
   - Runway estimation (months remaining)
   - Breakeven analysis

2. Capital Structure Analysis
   - Debt vs equity modeling
   - Cost of capital (APR comparison engine)
   - Opportunity cost calculator
   - Financing total cost projections
   - Fee drag analysis

3. Risk Management Core
   - Emergency reserve requirement calculator
   - Volatility tolerance mapping
   - Drawdown tolerance declaration
   - Capital partition enforcement
   - Stress test simulation (low revenue, high expense, delayed receivables)

4. Compliance & Governance Layer
   - Filing calendar tracking
   - Jurisdiction tagging
   - Audit log generation
   - Human approval checkpoints
   - Change-of-capital decision logging

5. Capital Partitioning
   - Personal Stability Capital (PSC)
   - Business Runway Capital (BRC)
   - Speculative Allocation Pool (SAP)
   - Immutable rule: PSC and BRC cannot be auto-drawn by speculative modules.

Telemetry Requirements:
-----------------------
Each capital decision must log:
   - Timestamp
   - Declared objective
   - Risk budget
   - Allocation category
   - Approval confirmation
   - Expected outcome
   - Post-outcome reflection

All logs append-only.
No silent mutation of prior entries.

--------------------------------------------------
LAYER 1: EXTENSION KIT FRAMEWORK
--------------------------------------------------

Purpose:
Allow modular experimentation under bounded risk conditions.

All Kits Must:
--------------
1. Be opt-in only.
2. Declare maximum capital allocation.
3. Inherit risk caps from FGK.
4. Enforce circuit breakers.
5. Require human confirmation for execution (v0.x phase).
6. Produce detailed telemetry logs.

Example Kits:

A. Speculative Trading Kit
   - Strategy declaration requirement
   - Position sizing engine
   - Max daily loss limit
   - Max drawdown halt
   - Slippage modeling
   - Regime tagging

B. Crypto Exposure Kit
   - Volatility-adjusted allocation
   - Exchange counterparty risk log
   - Custody method declaration

C. Options Strategy Kit
   - Defined-risk strategy requirement (no naked exposure in early phase)
   - Margin impact modeling
   - Expiration risk tracking

D. Venture Allocation Kit
   - Illiquidity risk declaration
   - Time horizon lock acknowledgement
   - Failure probability modeling

--------------------------------------------------
SAFETY INVARIANTS
--------------------------------------------------

1. No extension kit may exceed its capital partition.
2. No automated reallocation from runway capital.
3. Circuit breakers cannot be disabled without governance override.
4. Override events must generate elevated audit logs.
5. All probabilistic outputs must include variance ranges.

--------------------------------------------------
LEGAL POSITIONING
--------------------------------------------------

The system:
- Provides decision-support and modeling tools.
- Does not guarantee outcomes.
- Does not provide individualized legal or financial advice.
- Requires human authorization for execution.
- Maintains clear audit trails for accountability.

--------------------------------------------------
RESEARCH OBJECTIVE
--------------------------------------------------

Primary Research Question:
Does engrammitized financial governance reduce cognitive friction, 
decision latency, and capital misallocation for entrepreneurial operators?

Metrics:
- Decision clarity score (self-reported + structured)
- Error reduction rate
- Runway preservation delta
- APR avoidance savings
- Drawdown containment effectiveness
