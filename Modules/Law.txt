OAN LEGAL AWARENESS & GOVERNANCE MODULE
Constitutional Build Document v0.1
-----------------------------------

Purpose:
--------
To provide a structured Legal Awareness & Governance Module (LAGM) 
within the OAN Tech Stack. This module supports operators in understanding 
legal contexts, tracking obligations, documenting decisions, and maintaining 
procedural compliance awareness.

This system does not provide legal representation.
It scaffolds lawful conduct and structured awareness.

Philosophical Foundation:
-------------------------
1. Law precedes expansion.
2. Ignorance of law does not eliminate liability.
3. Documentation protects continuity.
4. Jurisdiction defines authority.
5. Human accountability is never removed.
6. All material legal decisions are engrammed append-only.

--------------------------------------------------
LAYER 0: LEGAL GOVERNANCE KERNEL (LGK)
--------------------------------------------------

Tone:
Institutional. Neutral. Jurisdiction-aware.
No interpretation beyond cited framework.
No advisory guarantees.

Core Functions:

1. Jurisdiction Mapping
   - Operator jurisdiction declaration
   - Federal / state / regional tagging
   - Industry-specific regulatory tagging
   - Cross-border activity identification

2. Obligation Tracking
   - Filing requirement checklist
   - Reporting duty tracker
   - Licensing maintenance calendar
   - Statutory deadline alerts
   - Renewal obligation reminders

3. Legal Risk Awareness
   - Contract necessity prompts
   - Liability exposure declaration logs
   - Insurance requirement awareness
   - Employment classification checks
   - Consumer protection compliance prompts

4. Documentation Discipline
   - Contract storage indexing
   - Amendment logging
   - Version tracking
   - Signature date registry
   - Communication archiving reference

5. Decision Logging
   - Legal context tag
   - Risk declaration statement
   - Human acknowledgment confirmation
   - Escalation recommendation flag
   - Amendment record capability

--------------------------------------------------
ENGRAM REQUIREMENTS
--------------------------------------------------

Each legal-context action must declare:

   - Jurisdiction
   - Applicable regulatory domain
   - Identified obligation
   - Risk category (low / moderate / high)
   - Human review confirmation
   - External counsel consultation flag (if applicable)

All entries append-only.
Corrections logged as amendments.
No silent deletion.

--------------------------------------------------
SAFETY INVARIANTS
--------------------------------------------------

1. The module shall not generate binding legal commitments autonomously.
2. All filings require human authorization.
3. High-risk actions require acknowledgment flag.
4. The system shall clearly indicate when professional counsel is recommended.
5. Jurisdictional ambiguity must trigger clarification prompt.

--------------------------------------------------
BOUNDARY CONDITIONS
--------------------------------------------------

The system:
   - Provides structured legal awareness support.
   - Does not provide legal advice.
   - Does not interpret statutes beyond informational summaries.
   - Does not replace licensed counsel.
   - Does not guarantee compliance outcomes.

--------------------------------------------------
RESEARCH OBJECTIVE
--------------------------------------------------

Primary Research Question:
Does engrammitized legal awareness reduce missed obligations,
documentation error, and preventable liability exposure for operators?

Metrics:
   - Missed filing reduction rate
   - Documentation completeness percentage
   - Liability incident reduction
   - Counsel escalation timing accuracy
   - Jurisdiction clarity score

--------------------------------------------------
ENGAGEMENT MODES & CREDENTIAL GATES
--------------------------------------------------

Mode A: Legal Awareness Mode (Default)
   - Explanatory summaries only.
   - No binding artifact creation.
   - No statutory interpretation beyond informational context.
   - No autonomous commitments.

Mode B: Assisted Draft Mode
   - Non-binding draft templates only.
   - Clear "DRAFT – NOT LEGAL ADVICE" watermark.
   - Human review and authorization required.
   - Elevated audit logging.

Mode C: Credential-Gated Mode
   - Activated only after verified credential documentation.
   - Role-bound to declared discipline and jurisdiction.
   - May assist a Competent Agent in structured drafting.
   - Does not assume legal liability.
   - All final authority remains with licensed professional.

--------------------------------------------------
UNAUTHORIZED PRACTICE OF LAW (UPL) SAFEGUARD
--------------------------------------------------

The module shall not:
   - Represent clients.
   - Provide jurisdiction-specific legal advice.
   - Interpret statutes beyond informational summaries.
   - Generate binding legal commitments autonomously.
   - Replace licensed counsel.

UPL-triggering activities require credential verification 
and human authorization.

--------------------------------------------------
ROLE DEFINITIONS
--------------------------------------------------

Operator:
   - Receives awareness support.
   - Approves filings manually.

Competent Agent:
   - Verified credential holder within a recognized discipline.
   - May unlock role-bound assistance features.
   - Retains full professional responsibility.

Executive Authority:
   - May authorize escalation.
   - Cannot override statutory compliance invariants.
