I. PURPOSE
============================================================

The Federated Governance Substrate (FGS) is a governance-first,
identity-bearing infrastructure designed to host intelligent systems
(LLMs, agents, swarms) without surrendering sovereignty,
semantic coherence, or jurisdictional accountability.

The FGS separates:

- Identity from Authority
- Capability from Permission
- Semantics from Policy
- Infrastructure from Persona

It is intended to scale from:

- Personal/Home Cradle
- Lab/Research Cradle
- Enterprise Cradle Networks
- Public-Sector Deployments
- Horizon Industrial Operations (including space-domain logistics)

============================================================

2. Cognitive Proximity (persistent session continuity)
3. Embodied Proximity (physical or humanoid chassis)
4. Social Proximity (integration into family, civic, or institutional structures)

- Authority must never be conflated with emotional proximity.
- Emotional support functions shall not grant operational authority.

- Role boundaries must remain explicit at all proximity levels.
- Higher proximity tiers require stricter locality, update control,
  logging transparency, and identity separation.

If governance maturity does not match proximity level,
the operational layer remains frozen.
